## Purpose

This tax statement was created by the taxpayer(s) using
OpenSteuerauszug (https://github.com/vroonhof/opensteuerauszug) from
electronic data of the financial institution. It serves to simplify
complete data entry and avoid typos.

## Data

Responsibility for accuracy and completeness still lies with the taxpayer.

- He/she checks holdings, transactions, Valor numbers and interest payments.
- The definitive tax calculation based on the Kursliste should be taken over by the tax software and/or assessment software.

All tax calculations in this file are based on the Kursliste and are for information purposes only.

## Why a tax statement?

Unfortunately there is no standardized way to import only position or
transaction data into PrivateTax or similar software.

Foreign financial institutions do not provide (e-)tax statements. The
author of the OpenSteuerauszug software believes that automated data
transfer is still beneficial, especially since tax software is able to
correct and/or complete the tax-relevant calculations.

## Requests / suggestions for improvement

The author hopes to make the work of all parties easier. Requests /
suggestions for improvement are accepted via GitHub.
